## Impact on SDK Users

Before this change, users importing the Temporal Go SDK would have these
dependencies in their go.mod due to Go's Minimal Version Selection:

- github.com/shirou/gopsutil/v4 and all its transitive deps
- github.com/go-ole/go-ole (Windows COM)
- github.com/tklauser/go-sysconf (syscall wrappers)
- github.com/yusufpapurcu/wmi (Windows WMI)
- go.einride.tech/pid (PID controller - not even used!)
- etc.

After this change, those dependencies are no longer required.
